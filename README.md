# **Supply Chain Management Analysis Report**

## **Executive Summary**
This analysis provides a comprehensive overview of the supply chain performance for a global e-commerce company. Through Python-based data analysis, key drivers of shipping delays and profitability have been identified, along with actionable recommendations to optimize delivery performance, reduce delays, and increase profitability.

## **Business Problem**
The company is facing challenges in meeting delivery schedules, leading to late deliveries and unpredictable profitability. The current shipping performance is inconsistent, and the company seeks to identify bottlenecks and improve the shipping process for better efficiency.

## **Skills**
- **Python Programming**
- **Data Visualization**
- **Supply Chain Management Analytics**
- **Predictive Modeling**
- **Business Insights Derivation**
- **Stakeholder Communication**

## **Methodology**
1. **Data Cleaning:** Raw order fulfillment and delivery data was pre-processed to ensure accuracy.
2. **Analysis:** Used Python and visualization tools to analyze delay patterns, profit margins, and order fulfillment times.
3. **Segmentation:** Split data by customer segment (Home Office, Consumer, Corporate) to uncover delayed delivery trends.
4. **Visualization:** Created visual representations for performance metrics and delay patterns.

## **Results**
- **Late delivery percentage:** 54.7%
- **Key delay drivers:** 1-day delays account for 31% of late orders.
- **Customer segments with the highest late delivery:** Home Office (55.3%), Consumer (54.6%).
- **Profitability Insights:** The average profit decreases with increasing delay days.
- **Orders delayed by 3+ days** showed substantial profit loss.

## **Recommendations**
1. **Implement predictive tools** to anticipate delays and adjust shipping plans proactively.
2. **Optimize operations for 1-day delays** as this is the most frequent.
3. **Address bottlenecks** in specific customer segments (Home Office, Consumer).
4. **Reduce unnecessary delays** by improving order preparation times.
5. **Enhance customer communication** regarding delivery times to improve trust.

## **Next Steps**
1. **Deploy a pilot program** to track real-time delays and adjust operations.
2. **Train customer support** to address delivery-related inquiries more effectively.
3. **Continue analyzing profit loss** per delay day to identify further optimizations.
4. **Implement data-driven decision-making tools** for operational adjustments.
